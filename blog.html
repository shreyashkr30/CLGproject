Do Car pOOl launches 11 Food/Refreshment Centers in Howrah


The summer wave is sweeping over India and has been taking a toll on the health of everyone around us. Do Car pOOl is highly concerned for the health of its captains who brave the sun throughout the day so that the customers reach their destinations quickly and safely. To replenish the energy of its captains, Do Car pOOl has taken an initiative to launch 11 refreshment and food centers throughout Bangalore. These centers shall provide healthy and nutritious food & juices at highly subsidized rates so that the captains don't have to burn a hole in their pockets, every time they feel famished after a hectic shift on the bike. The whole procedure is as simple as A-B-C. All they have to do is walk in to any of the centers, show their meal voucher in the Do Car pOOl Captain Application/SMS and get their food/refreshments! Utmost care is taken to ensure that the quality of the food/refreshments in these centers is good CENTERS:It gives the Do Car pOOl team immense satisfaction that this initiative is very well accepted and liked by our captains. Each captain is important to us and we owe our success to them. They are not just small dots on the map but our business partners without whom there can be no Do Car pOOl. This is just a small step in the direction of building a great partnership that will change the way India travels in the future!




Do Car pOOl rolls out its Power Pass Subscription Program


India's largest CAR taxi service, Do Car pOOl has launched its Power pass subscription program to make the daily commute more economical for its users. With the subscription, users can avail huge benefits on all their rides. Also, Pass users will be able to book their rides without any surge pricing levied on flat fares. What differentiates the Power pass from the other subscription programs is that the in-house developed tool suggests only one best suited pass for every individual user by analyzing the user's riding frequency and average riding distance.“We understand that every user is different and has unique preferences, which motivated us to develop one of a kind solution to cater to their needs. Our tool understands the user's behavior and provides the best suited pass rather than showing multiple passes and confusing them” Says Hiran Gunasekaran, Head Power Pass,Do Car pOOl.The subscription option has been rolled out to a limited set of customers across a few cities in India but it is expected to be made available to all Do Car pOOl customers in the coming months.Do Car pOol claims that the pass users are able to save up to 40% on their travel spendings. Also, the company claims that the pass users are riding 60% more frequently when compared to other users.Do Car pOOl aims to have 1 million active pass users by June 2020.

Breaking gender stereotypes


Achieving a gender-equal world starts by making sure women's needs/experiences are well integrated in the field of technology and innovations. Do Car pOOl believes that women can make a huge impact and here we give a big shout out to one of our woman captain - Manjula who started off her journey with Do Car pOOl as recent as last week and has gained popularity already.A 23 year old, based out of Bangalore - Manjula joined Do Car pOOl as a Captain on October 30, and on her very first day completed 7 rides. On meeting her and checking with her how she manages to be a female Car rider in today's world which is predominantly male dominated when it comes to deliveries, driving cabs etc, she expresses how important being independent is for her and why more number of females should be doing this. She further tells that she had great interactions with the customers and is also loving her job. Many of the pillion riders are actually very impressed with her professionalism and her navigation skills already.
